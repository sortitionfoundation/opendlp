Floating Alerts

In the backoffice, flash messages now appear as floating alerts at the bottom-right of the viewport. This ensures users see feedback without scrolling to the top of the page.

Test it: Visit /backoffice/dev/patterns?tab=floating-alerts to trigger test alerts interactively.
