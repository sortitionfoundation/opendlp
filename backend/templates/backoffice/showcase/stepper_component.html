{# Keyboard-nav info — the tabs-mode variant supports it #}

Keyboard navigation (tabs mode)

The stepper uses manual activation — arrow keys only move focus, they do not activate the step. Press Enter or Space to follow the focused step's link.

  • ← / → Move focus to previous/next step
  • Home / End First / last step
  • Enter / Space Activate the focused step
